Short Bio

Computational materials scientist focusing on microstructure-aware modeling for energy and electronics.

Bo Wang is an Assistant Research Professor at Penn State (MatSE). His work spans phase-field, FFT-based solvers, and multiscale frameworks applied to ferroelectrics and solid-state batteries.

Interests

  • Phase-field and mesoscale modeling
  • Ferroelectric microstructures and EO effects
  • Solid-state battery chemo-mechanics
